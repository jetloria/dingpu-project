Order Delivery Confirmation

For couriers and restaurants, getting the exact delivery confirmation date and time can be quite time-consuming and may have a lot of loopholes. When it comes to a non-biased delivery report confirmation, Pudding Tag's accurate date and time presence validation feature is a game changer.

Address and Presence Validation

Ironclad Proof of Asset Maintenance

Keep track of your precious assets and their scheduled upkeep. With a diverse range of equipment that requires frequent inspection, industries may struggle to manage and supervise them all simultaneously. Pudding Tags are a convenient and simple way to verify that maintenance or repairs were conducted on a certain piece of equipment on a particular day and time.

Efficient Roving Security and Border Patrol

For law enforcement or private security firms, the key to a successful roaming patrol is making accurate and timely site visits. Pudding tags can be extremely useful for comparing scheduled vs. real time reporting.

Asset Rental Management
