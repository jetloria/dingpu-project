Property Sector Management

With your roving security, ensure that every sector of your property is visited and checked during their shift. Install a Pudding Tag within a sector of your property and have your roving security activate the tag to provide an OTP that would return the time and date of the activation. Submission of this OTP by your security personnel ensures that they were present in that sector at the time of activation.

The Pudding Tag can be mounted or installed anywhere, as it is weatherproof, and does not require electricity or internet to work. Guaranteed to last 5 years.
