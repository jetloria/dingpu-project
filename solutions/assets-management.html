Assets Management

Pudding Tag can be used to keep track of maintenance records of your assets. Assign a Pudding tag on each asset that requires maintenance visits, and the tag will return an OTP that would return Date and Time of the activation. Submission of this OTP by your maintenance personnel ensures that they were physically present by the asset at the time of activation. Activating the Pudding Tag multiple times can indicate the start of the visit, and the end of the visit or maintenance.

The Pudding Tag can be mounted or installed anywhere, as it is weatherproof, and does not require electricity or internet to work. Guaranteed to last 5 years.
