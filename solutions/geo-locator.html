Geo Locator

Installation of Pudding Tags in public places will return a unique coordinate that can be used as a geographical reference for any business. The OTP generated by the tag will be submitted to return the Date, Time, and Exact Location of the device (accurate to a square meter). The OTP is to be submitted to the Pudding Portal that will return the information. Any company requiring logistical data can refer to the generated OTP to verify the exact location of the tag, thereby eliminating the need to obtain the address and or landmarks from the client.

The Pudding Tag can be mounted or installed anywhere, as it is weatherproof, and does not require electricity or internet to work. Guaranteed to last 5 years.
